About Me

Hi, I'm Rachel!

I'm a 25 year old native of Palmyra, Pennsylvania and I currently reside in Philadelphia with my goldendoodle, Fiona. I'm a student at UPenn LPS Coding Bootcamp and I am set to complete the program in November! Coding is relatively new for me. I decided to start this program because I am looking for a career change and also a new challenge



I got my undergraduate degree from Saint Joseph's University, where I majored in Communication Studies and double minored in Business and Food Marketing. I also was a high jumper on the track and field team.


What I Like to Do

Aside from coding, I am also a professional singer. I sing in a band called 'Philadelphia Funk Authority.' We perform most weekends at weddings, festivals, bars, and private parties. I started singing in 2018 when I auditioned for the role in the band. Before that, I was too afraid to showcase my skills and nobody knew that I could sing. Now, I've performed in front of thousands of people on stages all over the east coast. My biggest inspirations are Whitney Houston, Adele, Alicia Keys, and Aretha Franklin. Check out our video to hear my sound.

My other hobby is painting large-scale acrylic paintings. I love to paint with funky, vibrant colors. I've always been an artist, however I only started really pursuing this medium a few years ago. I love to express my creativity in wild ways!
